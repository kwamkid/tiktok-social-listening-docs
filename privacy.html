Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024
Important: This service only collects and analyzes publicly available TikTok content through official APIs. We do not access private user data, personal information, or private content.

1. Introduction

TikTok Social Listening ("we," "our," or "the Service") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our social listening and analytics service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 TikTok Content Data (Public Only)

Through TikTok's official Research API, we collect:

  • Public post content: Video descriptions, hashtags, captions
  • Public engagement metrics: Likes, comments, shares, views counts
  • Public creator information: Username, display name (no private details)
  • Metadata: Post creation time, video duration, music information

2.2 User Account Data

When you use our service, we may collect:

  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Username and password (encrypted)
  • Usage preferences and settings
  • Keywords you choose to monitor

2.3 Technical Data

  • IP address and browser information
  • Usage logs and analytics
  • Error logs for service improvement

3. What We DO NOT Collect

We explicitly DO NOT collect:
  • Private TikTok user profiles or content
  • Personal messages or private communications
  • Contact lists or social connections
  • Location data or device identifiers
  • Financial or payment information
  • Any content not publicly available on TikTok

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
